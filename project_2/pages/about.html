My name is Mollie.

I am a student at the University of Montana studying Journalism. I am passionate about travel, but traveling as a student isn't always the easiest thing. It costs a lot of time and money, which are two things that the average student does not have. However, out of all of my financial decisions insofar I have never regretted spending money on travel.

So far, I have traveled Germany, Austria, Hungary, Italy, France, the Netherlands, Canada and Mexico. I have plans to travel Berlin, The Phillipines, and Belgium this coming year.

I created this website for my Intro to Web Design Class for a project, but I decided on this topic because I love travel and have recently been doing more and more research on it and wanted to share my knowledge.

I get inspiration from a lot of different sources, but recently I have really enjoyed the YouTube Channel Vagabrothers. They are two brothers from California who travel the world, and have a lot of great videos about inexpensive places to travel, how to pack correctly, and different activities to do in different places.
